- **[GMOMETHODS](https://gmo-crl.jrc.ec.europa.eu/gmomethods/)** — A database of validated GMO detection methods developed and maintained for the EURL GMFF. Together with the companion tools GMO-Matrix and GMO-Amplicons, it is widely used by official GMO control laboratories across Europe, serving as a reference point for the European GMO analysis community.

- **[Science Radar](https://codeberg.org/emmecola/science-radar/)** — A multi-agent AI pipeline that produces illustrated essays on selected scientific topics, drawing from recent news and academic papers.

- **[sayansi.social](https://sayansi.social/)** — A Mastodon instance I host for the Italian science community, providing an independent space for science communication on the fediverse.
